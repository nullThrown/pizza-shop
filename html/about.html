About

My Story

I was eight years old the first time I throw a pizza dough ball into the air. I cannot say for certain whether I caught the ball upon its return but what it did, however, was cement a life-long love for pizza. I began working in my father's own restaurant at the ripe age of 16. There, I learned many of the family secrets surrounding pizza dough, marinara sauce, oven baking and loads more.

I opened Pizza Guys in 2004 with a desire to not only recreate my family's delicious recipes but to combine the results from my own experimentations as well. Many years later, our pizza still carries that authentic old school pizza taste combined with new styles and flavors not found anywhere else.

So, head on down to pizza guys where we live to serve pizza lovers like yourself!
